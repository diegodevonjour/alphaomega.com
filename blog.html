NODE WAR

Node Wars are the battles to gain ownership of these nodes. Once the node is occupied, the occupying guild will retain the ownership for a week, receiving part of the worldwide tax collection to fund their guild bank, which can be used for the growth of the guild later.

Anyone can freely join these wars regardless of their guild size or equipment/spec.
All you need to do is to work together with your team mates in harmony for victory.
